Early life

Kim Jong Un's early life was shrouded in mystery, with little public information available. He is believed to have attended school in Switzerland under an assumed name, where he reportedly showed an interest in basketball and other Western sports. After returning to North Korea, he is thought to have attended Kim Il Sung Military University.

Rise to Power

Kim Jong Un was groomed for leadership in the late 2000s. His father, Kim Jong Il, began to position him as his successor, despite Kim Jong Un being relatively young compared to his older brothers. In 2010, Kim Jong Un was promoted to high-ranking military and political posts, signaling his impending rise to power.
